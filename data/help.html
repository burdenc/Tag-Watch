Help

Why are some thumbnails grey?

Grey thumbnails signify a tag entry filtered because it was marked NSFW. You can turn on/off this filter per tag or per site.

Some thumbnails weren't greyed out even though I have the NSFW filter on!

My addon does nothing to check whether or not content is appropriate or not. It relies on the trust of the site you're using. Your trust in the NSFW filter should only be as strong as your trust in the tagging of NSFW on the targetted site. Basically don't count on the filter working for a community that's lousy at tagging content NSFW.

What sites are supported?

Wallbase and any site using the Gelbooru API

My site has stopped working!

Please let me know if a site stops working, this means the API or site layout has changed!
